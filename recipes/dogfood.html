Dog Food Recipe

Dog's Favorite Food

My dog loves this food and your dog will like it too!

Dog Food Ingredients

  • 1C Dog food (my dog weighs around 20lbs, adjust accordingly)

Dog Food Instructions

  1. Wake Up - Go to where you keep your dog food
  2. Get scoop, go into bag and fill 1 Cup of food (this is for dog that weights around 20lbs, adjust accordingly)
  3. Empty food into dog bowl, yell go eat!
  4. Your dog should be enjoying their food!
